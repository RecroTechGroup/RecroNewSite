Founder's Letter

At Recro, we are an innovative technology company rooted in Türkiye driven by a vision to expand globally. Our name, derived from the Latin word “Recro” means “to revive” and “to restore” This captures the essence of what we do: we bring new life and strength to businesses by blending artificial intelligence with a human-centered approach. Recro’s global vision is to bring our local expertise to the world stage by delivering future-ready solutions to organizations of all sizes. We offer technology to humanity with the philosophy of “Be inspired by the unknown” In every project, we move forward by considering not only our customers’ today but also their tomorrow. For us, what truly matters is using technology not merely as a tool, but as a means of creating value; making companies more competitive and more innovative. In short, at Recro, we are here to revive technology for the good of humanity. With our motto “Be inspired by the unknown” let’s shape the future together.

Zafer Küçükşabanoğlu, Founder of the RecroTech
